    Detailed Metrics (July–Dec 2025)

    • Client intakes: 8,615 total clients enrolled (6,426 by phone; 2,189 web).
    • App installs: 1,783 new users (Android + iOS).
    • Online Video Intervention views: 9,121 unique views across 9 videos.
    • Quit kits distributed: 22,610 total (86.7% digital delivery).
    • Counseling: 5,847 clients received counseling; 69 certificates of enrollment sent.
    • Website traffic: 175,163 new users; top pages: Quit Now, Homepage, Quit Smoking, Our Program, Quit Vaping.
    • NRT kits distributed: 2,457 NRT kits to eligible clients.
    • 211 referrals (July–Dec 2025): 11,689 unique referrals; 6,582 reached (57%); 3,337 enrolled; 2,339 completed counseling (70% of enrolled).
