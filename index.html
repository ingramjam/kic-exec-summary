Detailed Program Results (July–Dec 2025)

Text Service
  • 2,719 unique users enrolled in the automated text-based cessation service to quit smoking or vaping.
  • 90% attributed to CTCP, 10% to CDC Quitline Supplement.
  • Tracking measure 1-2-5 Text Report includes utilization and language breakdown.
  • Excludes Asian Smokers' Quitline (ASQ) clients.
Chat Service
  • 458 unique chat sessions provided to clients.
  • Trending slightly below annual goal due to reduced media campaign.
  • 90% attributed to CTCP, 10% to CDC.
  • Tracking Measure 1-2-7 Chat Report includes utilization details.
Client Intakes
  • 8,615 total clients enrolled (6,426 by phone: 90% English, 10% Spanish; 2,189 web: 95% English, 5% Spanish).
  • 50% of phone clients heard about KIC via Mass Media/Advertising.
  • Excludes First 5 & ASQ clients (funded by other grants).
  • Tracking measure 1-2-8 Intake Report includes client demographics.
App
  • 1,783 new users (Android + iOS), on track to surpass annual goal of 2,400.
  • Majority of app traffic is organic; some paid search campaign support.
  • 90% attributed to CTCP, 10% to CDC.
Online Video Intervention
  • 9,121 unique views across 9 videos (8 quit smoking, 1 proxy-focused).
  • Videos available in English and Spanish on KIC’s YouTube channel.
  • Exceeded annual goal of 2,400 unique views by 6,721 views.
  • 90% attributed to CTCP, 10% to CDC.
Quit Kits
  • 22,610 quit kits sent to clients; 86.7% delivered digitally via email.
  • Physical kits fulfilled by TECC; MOU to be reviewed after new SOW in July 2026.
  • Excludes First 5 & ASQ clients.
  • 90% attributed to CTCP, 10% to CDC.
Counseling
  • 5,847 tobacco users or proxies received counseling in English or Spanish via telephone.
  • On track to exceed annual goal; 69 certificates of enrollment sent.
  • Excludes First 5 & ASQ clients.
  • 90% attributed to CTCP, 10% to CDC.
Website
  • 175,163 new users visited the KIC website (89% English, 11% Spanish).
  • Largest source of new traffic: Paid Search (67,878 new users, 39% of total traffic).
  • Top 5 most visited pages: Quit Now, Homepage, Quit Smoking, Our Program, Quit Vaping.
  • Most popular CTAs: Enroll online (Quit Now), Click to Chat.
  • SEO and content updates, new marijuana page, HubSpot CRM migration prep.
NRT Kits
  • 2,457 NRT kits distributed to eligible clients.
  • See tracking measures for NRT Distribution report and eligibility requirements.
211 Referrals
  • 11,689 unique referrals sent to KIC (July–Dec 2025), averaging 1,948/month (goal: 1,666/month).
  • 6,582 reached (57%), 3,337 enrolled, 2,339 completed counseling (70% of enrolled).
  • See 3-1-3 Final for 211 Call Center Overview and Breakdown.
Media & Communications
  • 6 CTPP Partners updates, 5 e-blasts/newsletters, 2 InfoHub posts, 4 UNDO Together Call announcements.
  • 306 unpaid social posts (51/month), 111,776 reach, 134 new followers, 801 interactions, 655 shares.
  • 6 Meta ad campaigns: 1.5M+ impressions, 34k clicks, 15,604 CTA conversions.
  • Promotional flyers in Medi-Cal mailings, tracked by promo/QR code.
  • Monthly meetings with CTPP Media Team, new communication assets shared.
